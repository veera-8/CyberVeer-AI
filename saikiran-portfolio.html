About Me

From Hacker
to Builder

I'm a 16-year-old cybersecurity researcher, AI builder, and founder from Rajam, Andhra Pradesh. While most kids were in school doing homework, I was discovering critical vulnerabilities in live production systems — and getting recognized for it.

My journey started with pure curiosity: understanding how systems break. That obsession evolved into building systems that don't break. Today, I channel that energy into CyberVeer AI and Guidonship — two startups solving real problems with intelligent technology.

Beyond screens, I'm a pencil carving artist — precision in code translates to precision in art. I believe the best hackers and the best artists think the same way: obsessively.
